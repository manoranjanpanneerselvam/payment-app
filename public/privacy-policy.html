Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

My Payment App ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our payment services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment transaction details (order ID, payment ID)
  • IP address and browser information
  • Device information used to access our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and verify your payments
  • Send transaction confirmation and receipts
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our services

3. Payment Security

All payments are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store your card details or banking credentials on our servers. All transactions are encrypted using 256-bit SSL technology.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with:

  • Razorpay (payment processing)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain transaction records for a period of 5 years as required by Indian financial regulations. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.

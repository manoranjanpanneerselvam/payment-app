Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

At My Payment App, we are committed to ensuring a fair and transparent refund process. Please read this policy carefully before making a payment.

1. Refund Eligibility

A refund may be issued in the following cases:

  • Payment was debited but transaction was not confirmed
  • Duplicate payment was made for the same transaction
  • Technical error caused incorrect amount to be charged

2. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Successfully completed transactions where service was delivered
  • Payments cancelled by the user after processing
  • Transactions declined by the bank or payment gateway

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Email us at manoranjan98p@gmail.com with your transaction/order ID
  • Include your payment details and reason for refund
  • We will review and respond within 3-5 business days

4. Refund Timeline

Once a refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method. Bank processing times may vary.

5. Cancellation Policy

Payments once initiated cannot be cancelled mid-transaction. If a payment fails, no amount will be deducted. If deducted in error, it will be automatically refunded within 5-7 business days.

6. Contact for Refunds

Email: manoranjan98p@gmail.com
Please allow 1-2 business days for a response.
